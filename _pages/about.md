# About Me

I am a first-year PhD candidate at the [HKUST NLP Group](https://cse.hkust.edu.hk/~jhe/), supervised by Professor Junxian He. My research focuses on Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Machine Learning, with particular interests in LLM Reasoning, Reinforcement Learning, Hallucination in Vision-Language Models (VLM), LLM truthfulness, and Interpretability.
